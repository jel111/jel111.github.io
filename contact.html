Stay in Touch!

Please feel free to contact us.

Here at Neighborsit we really, really, really would like to hear your feed back. At the bottom of is page is a contact form and we would truly appreciate if you were to contact us. It can be just to say "Yo What Up" or to tell us how much of an awesome job we are doing! Of course there is the dreaded you are unhappy, but we need to hear it all. The only way we can make Neighborsit better is if we hear and listen to our users.

Please contact us with the form below to let us know the reason of your inquiry. We will then be able to address you issue or praise and know what to do next. We are especially interested your feedback in these early stages of developement. All of your input helps! If you have design or functionality concerns or we are missing a feature you would like to see please feel free to contact us. Are we doing something that you think we can do better? Should we subtract something that drives you crazy? All messages are relevant to us and we will go through each one.

To be clear, we will not sell you email address or even give it to anyone. We do reserve the right to contact you with follow up questions about your inquiry or to get a more in-depth explanation. Thanks so much for taking the time out of your day to help us make Neighborsit better. Have a great day from all of us at neighborsit.com!

Questions or comments? Get in touch:

Please feel free to contact about any question or ideas you may have related to Neighborsit. We are always open to ideas on how to improve our App and website. Our goal is to be a community driven source for people to help people. For neighborhoods to be taken back and not allowed to be just a bunch of houses where no one knows anybody. If you have a thought on how we can do better or would like to share how much you enjoy Neighborsit please do! Use the form and we promise not to sell your email address!
