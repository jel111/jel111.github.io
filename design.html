Neighborsit Design

On this page we are going to show the life cycle of our design. We are hoping to get help from the community with that process. I think this will greatly improve not only the look and feel of the App but the whole functionality. This app will not only be a community driven but community designed application and website.

In the near future we are going to be running contests to see who can come up with the best designed button or the best designed logo. Maybe we will even go as far as the best designed page!

We will hopefully get that far. As we stand right now the feedback that we desparately need is about how this app is seen to other people. What I mean by this is when you heard the name, NeighborSit, and then got a glimpse at what it is supposed to do what were your first thoughts. I would love it if you could leave those thoughts in the form below.

Why are we asking the community?

Neighbor sit is a community driven app. Without a community behind this app it goes absolutely nowhere. So why not have our neighbors help us design this app? We are the ones that will use it and we are the ones that know what we want out of this app. Sure our team knows what we think this app should do but we are really interested in what our neighbors think.

I don't know if you have noticed but my favorite websites and apps as of late have been asking there community or "Neighbors" what they think. What they would do to the app or website to make it better in their eyes. As the old saying goes two Neighbors are better than one! Ok so I took a little liberty with that sayong but you get the point. I will say again that we need our neighbors input in this matter. Sometimes you can get so close to something that you just need a new set of eyes on it. They will most times make you aware of that elephant in the room. We may have forgotten something so simple, so glaringly obvious that everybody thinks why didn't they do such and such? Us being so close to this day after day makes us oblivious to the simple stuff. Any and all suggetions are very much appreciated. Please, just be kind and respectful because we have feelings and are not just a screen on a desk with no emotions and a computer voice.

Thank you very much for taking time out of your busy day to give us a hand!

Please don't forget about the form at the bottom of this page. You can just say hi and we will sign you up to receive updates on how things are going and of our release date. We will not give anyone your email address or information and if you state you do not want to receive updates just a release notification we will honor that too. The updates will be no more than one a week unless we have big news like, contest winners or something along those lines. Thanks again for being a great neighbor and have an awesome day!

Questions or comments? Get in touch:

Please feel free to contact about any question or ideas you may have related to Neighborsit. We are always open to ideas on how to improve our App and website. Our goal is to be a community driven source for people to help people. For neighborhoods to be taken back and not allowed to be just a bunch of houses where no one knows anybody. If you have a thought on how we can do better or would like to share how much you enjoy Neighborsit please do! Use the form and we promise not to sell your email address!
