  • What is our design pattern.

    We actually are novices in the design department and have no design pattern as of yet. Although we know we should, we have yet to instill one. We are building our stylesheet as we speek and are just trying to keep in a playful mood. We think it should be friendly colors that emit happy thoughts! Thoughts of what it would be like if most neighbors were friends, worried about one another and willing to help make our communities the best they can be.
